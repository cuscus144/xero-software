How it works

in milliseconds

01

Multi-market scanning

Our AI engine monitors thousands of instruments across crypto, synthetic indices, equities, and energy markets simultaneously — 24 hours a day.

Proprietary machine learning models analyze order flow, market structure, and momentum indicators to surface only the highest-confidence setups.

03

Automated execution

Approved signals are routed to your connected broker within milliseconds. Entry, stop-loss, and take-profit levels are calculated and placed atomically.

Position-level and portfolio-level risk controls run continuously. The system auto-adjusts or halts trading if drawdown thresholds are reached.
